## VW Bug Ranch
This was an easier driving day augmented with a few fun stops. Our first stop was the VW Bug Ranch - a very close descendent of the Cadillac Ranch. The advantage is that this stop was far less busy and the kids had a blast painting and enjoying themselves. Our next stop was the Tower Conoco / U-Drop Inn. This building was the inspiration for Ramone's detail shop in the movie Cars. We had a fun time looking around and I particularly enjoyed the fact that there was a bank of Tesla charging stations out back... the mix of new technology with the old was really something to see. 

We arrived early at the hotel in Oklahoma City and spent a good bit of time in the pool - a great finish for the day. Our plans for tomorrow are in a bit of flux as our our planned stop is closed, but we'll figure something out.
