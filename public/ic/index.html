Pages of Interest

Regular Attenders

Combined view of members (dark blue) and regular attenders (light blue). Immanuel Church locations marked in red with 2.5, 5, and 10 mile radius markings in green.

Regular Attenders (Clustered)

Combined view of members (dark blue) and regular attenders (light blue). Pins are automatically clustered by geographic proximity. Zooming in/out gives insight into relative distances. NOTE: hovering over larger-clusters (> 2 pins) will provide an outline of the area covered by that cluster.

Regular Attenders (Heat Map)

Combined view of members and regular attenders in a heatmap. Zooming in/out conveys relative "hot spots".

Regular Attenders By ZipCode

Combined view of members and regular attenders grouped by zip code. Scale is indicated in upper right of map. Hovering over each zipcode provides zipcode-specific details.

Other Churches

Immanuel Church locations (red) with 2.5, 5, and 10 mile radius marked in green. Other churches of interest in the area with individual 5-mile radii marked in orange. Zooming in/out give detail/separation.

IC, West Park, Regulars

Immanuel Church locations with 5 mile radius marked in green. West Park location marked with 5 mile radius marked in orange. Regular attenders with no prior affiliation with WestPark are indicated with green. Regular attenders with recent West Park affiliation are marked in orange. Those with long-past affiliation with West Park are marked in light green. The menu on the upper right allows layers to be enabled/disabled.

Community Group Meeting Locations

Meeting locations for Community Groups. If Virtual, use leader's residence as location.

Community Group Area Coverage

Shows geographic coverage for each of the community groups based on participants. Menu on upper right allows you to turn on/off individual groups.
All maps support zoom in/out (try mouse wheel). Clicking on pins should show name/title of that location.
