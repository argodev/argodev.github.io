## Big Driving Day

Today's schedule was not all that exciting: drive all day. That said, we had a great time. We left town a little later than planned (Matt & I went for a run) but were off by about 10:15. We stopped at the TN/VA border for a picinic lunch and then continued what turned out to be a beautiful drive up I-81. The weather was sunny and clear, providing a great view of the mountains in the distance the majority of the way. Once we got settled at the hotel, we found a local resturant to try out and, while not a 5-star establishment, it was just what we were looking for and had decent GF options. Tomorrow we start with Gettysburg...
